About World Hotels

Our Story

A Journey of Excellence

World Hotels began in 1988 with a single boutique hotel in London and has grown into a global collection of luxury properties. We are dedicated to providing excellent service, paying attention to every detail, and creating memorable experiences for our guests.

Today, we continue to lead in luxury hospitality, offering a blend of timeless elegance and modern innovation in our carefully selected hotels and resorts around the world.
